TRAINS
SHATABDI CONNECTING TWO CITY CENTERS IN DIFFERENT STATES. IT IS THE FASTEST GROUP OF TRAINS IN INDIA. IT IS FULLY AIR CONDITIONED WITH EXECUTIVE CHAIR CAR AND AC CHAIR CAR
RAJDHANI EXPRESS IT CONNECTS A STATE CAPITAL WITH CITY CAPITAL. THESE SUPERFAST TRAINS ARE FULLY AIR CONDITIONED AND WITH FEW HALTS. THE FIRST RAJDHANI WAS BETWEEN HOWRAH AND NEW DELHI IN 1989.
DURONTO EXPRESS THESE ARE NON STOP EXPRESS TRAINS WHICH RUNS IN SHORT AND LONG HAWLS. IT HAVE BOTH AC AND NON AC COMPARTMENTS.
SUPERFAST EXPRESS THESE TRAINS RUN IN LONG DISTANCE WITH HALTS IN IMPORTANT CITY CENTERS ONLY. IT USUALLY RUNS WITH WAP4/5/7 OR WDP 3A/4/4D
JAN SHATABDI THESE ARE SUPERFAST INTERCITY EXPRESS TRAINS WHICH ONLY STOPS IN MAXIMUM OF 3 CITY CENTERS IN A DISTRICT WITH A MINIMUM DISTANCE OF 50KMS APART. THE TRAIN WILL RUN B/W DESTINATIONS WITHIN 1000 KMS
EXPRESS TRAINS THESE ARE LIMITED STOP TRAINS WHICH RUNS OVER A SHORT OR LONG DISTANCES. IT USES WAP 4 AND WDP 3A ENGINES
PASSENGER TRAINS THESE TRAINS ONLY HAVE GENERAL 2S COMPARTMENTS WHICH ARE NOT INTERCONNECTED. IT RUNS B/W VERY SHORT DISTANCES WITH HAVING STOPAGES IN ALL THE STATIONS INTERMEDIATING. IT INCLUDES MEMU, DMU AND OTHER LOCAL TRAINS
